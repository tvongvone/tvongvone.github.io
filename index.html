Thanks for checking out my portfolio! Alittle bit about myself and why I decided to pursue a career as a Software Engineer. Being raised around video games for a majority of my life, I naturally gravitated towards programming and anything tech related. I remember when I first started coding, I noticed that gaming and programming shared the same nuances. I had to apply creative problem solving skills and that was something that I always loved about video games that persists. That's why I decided to become a Software Engineer and attended App Academy where I learned the most popular programming languages and tools for modern web applications like React, Redux, Javascript, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy and many more! Outside of programming my hobbies include taking my dog on long walks, boxing, learning new programming languages, and annoying my cat :)
